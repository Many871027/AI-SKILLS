# Translating ML Metrics to Business (Lean Analytics)
Never report just "Accuracy". Always tie metrics back to business value:

* **High Precision Focus:** Optimizes for reducing False Positives. *Business Translation:* Reduces wasted budget, prevents customer friction from false fraud alerts, and ensures high confidence in targeted marketing.
* **High Recall Focus:** Optimizes for reducing False Negatives. *Business Translation:* Critical for risk mitigation, medical diagnosis, churn prediction, and catching anomalies before they cause systemic failure.
* **F1-Score:** The harmonized mean. Use this as the primary champion metric when the business cost of False Positives and False Negatives is roughly equal.
